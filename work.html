Regulated client · 2025

A closed-domain assistant that refuses to guess.

We built an assistant that answers inside a defined domain and hands everything else to a person, on purpose. It declines questions it cannot ground in source, rather than inventing an answer.

What it does and does not do. It answers questions it can ground in source, with citations. It refuses questions it cannot ground, and routes them to a human. We set that line deliberately, and we can show you exactly where it sits and why.

No language model has literally zero error. The honest design is a system that refuses outside its domain, and you can see where the line is.
