AI engineering

Honest about what it can and cannot do

AI is a fast-growing part of what we do, and we build it the same way we build everything else: to run in production, and to be honest about what it can and cannot do. We can take something from a rough first prototype all the way to a tool your team uses every day, and we work in the open so you always know how it reaches its answers.

What we build
  • Chatbots and assistants. Conversational tools that answer from your own documents and data, for customer support, internal knowledge, or anything in between, with a source behind every answer.
  • Retrieval and grounded answers. Systems that look things up in your own material before they respond, so answers are based on what you actually know rather than guesswork.
  • Agent and harness engineering. The scaffolding around a model that lets it use tools, call your APIs and work through multi-step tasks reliably, plus the test harnesses that keep it honest as it changes.
  • Workflow automation. Wiring AI into the real work, so routine steps like triage, drafting, data entry and routing happen on their own, with a person checking the edges that matter.
  • MCP servers and integrations. We connect AI tools to your own systems through the Model Context Protocol, the emerging standard for letting an assistant safely act in your tools rather than just talk about them.
  • SDKs and developer tooling. Libraries and tools so your own engineers can build on top of what we set up, instead of depending on us for every change.
  • Evaluation, deployment and monitoring. Pipelines that measure whether the system is actually right, deployment on-prem or in sovereign cloud where your data is allowed to live, and monitoring that keeps watching after launch.

How it goes

How working
with us goes

We try to keep things simple and honest. We meet you in person for the first conversation, usually in Gothenburg or Stockholm. From there we write down exactly what we are going to do, what done looks like, and what it costs, as one fixed price rather than an open meter. Then we build it, keep you in the loop as we go, and hand it over with the documentation to maintain it. If you get in touch, you will hear back within one working day.

Who we are

A small team,
on purpose.

Vynatix is a small software consultancy in Gothenburg, working since 2023 and part of Ultra Group. We keep the team small on purpose, never more than fifteen senior engineers at once, so the people you meet are the people doing the work.

We started Vynatix because we had seen how most consultancies sell hours, and the hours billed rarely match the hours that actually move a project forward. We wanted to work a different way: agree on an outcome, price it as fixed work, and write it all down so the next person can pick it up.

We have our main office on Magasinsgatan in Gothenburg and a second desk on Birger Jarlsgatan in Stockholm, where a couple of the team are based. We like meeting clients face to face for that first conversation. After that, most of the work happens in writing, which keeps everyone clear on what was agreed.
