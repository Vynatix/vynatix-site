Services

Three things, done very well.

We do three things very well. Anything outside them, we decline and point you to someone better. The work is fixed in scope before it starts and priced against an outcome, a deployed system, an audit passed, a build that stays green, not against a body count.

01

AI engineering

We build production systems on foundation models, not slideware. That means retrieval that returns the right context, evaluation pipelines that tell you when the model is wrong, deployment into your environment including on-prem and sovereign cloud, and observability so you can see what the system is doing after it ships.

We will tell you plainly what a model can and cannot do for your problem. If a simpler tool would serve you better than a foundation model, we will say so before you spend on one.

What we build
  • Retrieval-augmented generation
  • Evaluation pipelines
  • On-prem and sovereign cloud deployment
  • Production observability
02

Cybersecurity

We do threat modelling, penetration testing, and incident response. Our reports pass audit because they were written to evidence every finding and pair it with a fix, not to look thorough.

The deliverable is the write-up. You get the findings, the evidence, the severity, and the remediation, in language your auditor and your engineers can both act on.

What we do
  • Threat modelling
  • Penetration testing
  • Incident response
  • NIS2 and ISO 27001 readiness
03

Cloud platforms

We build and run cloud platforms for regulated industries, on GCP, AWS, and on-prem hybrids. The goal is boring infrastructure: pipelines that stay green, deployments you can reason about, and a bill that matches what you actually use.

What we build
  • Platform engineering
  • Kubernetes and GitOps
  • FinOps and cost control
  • Migration off legacy systems
